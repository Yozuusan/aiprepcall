CASEBOOKS DIRECTORY
===================

Place your consulting case interview PDF files here.

Supported formats:
- Text-based PDFs (not scanned images)
- Casebooks from McKinsey, BCG, Bain
- Business school casebooks (Darden, Kellogg, etc.)

After adding PDFs:
1. Run: python3 backend/extractPDFs.py
2. Run: node backend/buildKnowledgeBase.js
3. Restart the server

The more PDFs you add, the better the quality of generated cases!
